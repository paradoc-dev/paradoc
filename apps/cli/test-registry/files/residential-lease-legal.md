## 2. TERM OF LEASE

The lease term shall commence on **{{leaseStartDate}}** and shall terminate on **{{leaseEndDate}}** ("Lease Term"), unless sooner terminated in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

## 3. RENT

### 3.1 Monthly Rent
Tenant agrees to pay Landlord monthly rent in the amount of **${{monthlyRent}}** (the "Rent"). Rent shall be due and payable on the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Lease Term.

### 3.2 Late Payment
If Rent is not received by Landlord by the fifth (5th) day of any month, Tenant shall pay a late fee equal to five percent (5%) of the monthly Rent.

### 3.3 Method of Payment
Rent shall be paid by check, money order, or electronic transfer to the account designated by Landlord.

## 4. SECURITY DEPOSIT

### 4.1 Amount
Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall pay to Landlord a security deposit in the amount of **${{securityDeposit}}** (the "Security Deposit").

### 4.2 Purpose
The Security Deposit shall be held by Landlord as security for the faithful performance by Tenant of all terms, covenants, and conditions of this Agreement.

### 4.3 Return of Deposit
Within thirty (30) days after termination of this Agreement and Tenant's vacating of the Premises, Landlord shall return the Security Deposit to Tenant, less any deductions for unpaid rent, damages beyond normal wear and tear, and cleaning costs if the Premises are not left in the same condition as at the commencement of the Lease Term.

## 8. USE OF PREMISES

The Premises shall be used solely for residential purposes and shall be occupied only by the Tenant(s) named in this Agreement.

## 9. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

### 9.1 Landlord Responsibilities
Landlord shall maintain the Premises in compliance with all applicable housing codes and shall make necessary repairs to maintain the Premises in habitable condition.

### 9.2 Tenant Responsibilities
Tenant shall maintain the Premises in clean and sanitary condition and shall not cause damage to the Premises beyond normal wear and tear.

## 10. ENTRY BY LANDLORD

Landlord may enter the Premises with at least twenty-four (24) hours prior notice for the purpose of inspection, repairs, or showing the Premises to prospective tenants or buyers.

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 End of Term
This Agreement shall terminate at the end of the Lease Term unless renewed by mutual written agreement of the parties.

### 11.2 Early Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement prior to the end of the Lease Term by providing sixty (60) days written notice to the other party.

## 12. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state in which the Premises are located.
